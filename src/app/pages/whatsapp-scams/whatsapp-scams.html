WhatsApp Scams: How to Stay Safe

Common WhatsApp Scams

  • Account Takeover Scams: Scammers pretend to be WhatsApp support asking for your verification code. Never share your 6-digit code with anyone.
  • Friend/Family Impersonation: Scammers hack accounts and message contacts asking for money or gift cards.
  • Prize/Lottery Scams: Messages claiming you've won a prize or lottery, asking for personal information or fees.
  • Job Offer Scams: Fake work-from-home opportunities requiring upfront payments or personal information.

Warning Signs

  • Urgent requests for money or personal information
  • Messages with spelling or grammar errors
  • Requests to move conversations off WhatsApp
  • Unknown contacts claiming to be from official organizations
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers

Protection Tips

  • Enable two-step verification in WhatsApp settings
  • Verify suspicious messages by calling the person directly
  • Never share your verification code or PIN
  • Block and report suspicious contacts
  • Be skeptical of unexpected messages, even from known contacts
